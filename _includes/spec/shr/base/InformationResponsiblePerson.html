InformationResponsiblePerson

The person who is responsible for (and may certify) the content of the entry.
Value: Choice
        | CareProfessional 1 A person who practices a healing art.
        | FamilyMember 1 A person related to the subject of record by blood, adoption, or marriage.
        | UnrelatedThirdParty 1 A person, other than a professional care provider or family member, who is relevant to the health or social situation of the subject.
