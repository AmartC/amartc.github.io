Entry

An item inserted in an electronic record C1705654
Meta 1 Contains elements common to all entries, describing the identification, timing, attribution, and other properties of the entry.
ShrID 1 A unique, persistent identifier for the Standard Health Record to which this entry belongs.
FocalSubject 1 The person or thing that this entry refers to, usually the Person of Record. However, not all entries refer to the Person of Record. The entry could refer to a fetus, care giver, or relative (living or dead)
SubjectIsThirdParty 1 A flag indicating this entry concerns someone other than the Person of Record
Summary optional A shortened version of an item of information, containing only the main points.
