Entry

Metadata elements added to entries in the SHR, describing the identification, timing, attribution, and other circumstances involved in an SHR entry.
EntryID 1 A unique, persistent, permanent identifier for an entry in a health record
StandardHealthRecordID 1 The persistent, unique ID of this standard health record
PersonOfRecordID 1 The persistent, unique ID of the person described by and tracked by this record. This ID has no intrinsic meaning and should not contain any identifiable information, such a mashup of name and birth date.
FocalSubject 1 The person or thing that this entry refers to. Note that not all entries refer to the Subject of SHR (the Person of Record). For example, the entry could refer to a fetus, care giver, or relative (living or dead)
EncounterOfEntry optional The encounter or episode of care that led to creation of this entry
DateEntered 1 The point in time when the information was recorded in the system of record.
DateUpdated 0 or more A date that the entry was changed
Informant 0 or more From whom or what the information originates, as distinct from the actor creating the entry, e.g. the subject (patient), medical professional, family member, device or software program.
InformationResponsiblePerson 0 or more The person who is responsible for (and may certify) the content of the entry.
Language optional A human language, spoken or written
Summary optional A shortened version of an item of information, containing only the main points.
Version optional A string identifying the particular of form of something (such as a code system or software product) that is different in some way from another form of the same thing.
