TestResult [Entry]

The result of a laboratory, radiologic, or other clinical test or measurement performed to determine the presence, absence, or degree of a condition or assess the health of an individual. C0456984
Value: Choice
        | Quantity 1 A measured or measurable amount
        | Coding 1 Coding of a concept, drawn from a controlled vocabulary. Includes the vocabulary and version, if applicable. May include a display text, and a descriptor expressing the intended interpretation of the code
Test 1 A laboratory, radiologic, or other clinical test or measurement used to determine the presence, absence, or degree of a condition or assess the health of an individual. Includes patient-generated data, such as pedometer readings.
Performer 1 or more An actor who performs a certain task or activity. Unlike a HealthcareInvolvement which continues over period of time, the performer is associated with a specific task, such admitting a patient, performing a procedure, or taking a measurement
Choice 1
        | TestTime 1 The time at which a measurement was taken or specimen collected.
        | TestPeriod 1 The start and end times for a measurement, if observed over a period of time, or the start and end of the specimen collection, if collected over a significant period of time (e.g. 24 hour Urine Sodium).
TestInterpretation optional A clinical interpretation of a measurement
ChangeFlag optional Indicator of change from the last or previous measurement.
TestRequest optional A record of a request for a diagnostic investigation service to be performed.
