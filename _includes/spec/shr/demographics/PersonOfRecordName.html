PersonOfRecordName [Entry]

A name used by the person of record who is the subject of this Standard Health Record, aka the patient. C1299487, 54125-0
Value: HumanName A full name for a human being, written as it would be typically expressed. May include a breakdown of the various elements of the name (family name, given name, etc.)
HumanNameUse 0 or more A qualifier specifying how a human name is used by a person, such as a legal name, alias, or nickname.
EffectivePeriod optional The date and time span for which something is active, valid, or in force.
