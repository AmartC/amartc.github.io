Encounter

The SHR Encounter domain contains definitions that capture interactions between the person of record and healthcare providers, including inpatient, ambulatory care, and telecare.

Encounter [Entry]

A statement about a planned or actual interaction between a patient and healthcare provider(s) for the purpose of providing healthcare service(s) or assessing the health status of a patient.
