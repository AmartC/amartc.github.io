MedicationNotAdministered [Entry]

A record of a medication not prescribed or administered. Recorded only when deviating from the normal expectation, care plan, or standard of care.
Medication 1 A type of prescription drug or over-the-counter drug that is used to prevent, treat, or relieve symptoms of a disease or abnormal condition, but excluding vaccines.
ReasonForUse optional The rationale for why the patient receives a medication or prescription
ReasonMedicationNotGiven 1 The rationale for NOT using particular medication treatment
