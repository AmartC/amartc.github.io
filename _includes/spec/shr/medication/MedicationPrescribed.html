MedicationPrescribed [Entry]

Report of a medication prescription.
Medication 1 A type of prescription drug or over-the-counter drug that is used to prevent, treat, or relieve symptoms of a disease or abnormal condition, but excluding vaccines.
UsedOrNotUsed 1 Whether the medication stated was used or not used
        with Coding is prescribed Coding of a concept, drawn from a controlled vocabulary. Includes the vocabulary and version, if applicable. May include a display text, and a descriptor expressing the intended interpretation of the code
ReasonForUse 1 The rationale for why the patient receives a medication or prescription
ReasonMedicationNotGiven 1 to 0 The rationale for NOT using particular medication treatment
Dosage optional The dosage of the medication as prescribed
Adherence 0 A statement of the ability and cooperation of the patient in taking medicine or supplement as recommended or prescribed. This includes correct timing, dosage, and frequency.
OccurrenceTime 1 The point in time or span of time in which something happens.
