MedicationStrength

Specifies how many (or how much) of the active ingredient there is in this Medication, to be interpreted as a ratio. For example, 250 mg per tablet. This is expressed as a ratio where the numerator is 250mg and the denominator is 1 tablet. C3176062
ActiveIngredientAmount 1 The amount of an active ingredient in a medication
AmountOfMedication 1 A standardized measure or discrete amount of medication serving as a reference for dosing or strength, for example, 1 tablet.
