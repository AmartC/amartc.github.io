HIVRapidScreeningTestRequest [Entry]
Based On: TestRequest A record of a request for a diagnostic investigation service to be performed.
Test 1 or more A laboratory, radiologic, or other clinical test or measurement used to determine the presence, absence, or degree of a condition or assess the health of an individual. Includes patient-generated data, such as pedometer readings.
RequestStatus 1 The extent to which the ordering process has progressed, for this order.
EffectivePeriod 1 The date and time span for which something is active, valid, or in force.
TestTiming optional When test or tests should be done. If the tests are a series or recur (e.g. daily blood sugar testing in the morning) then a Timing can be used to describe the periodicity.
PriorityOfRequest optional Urgency level for which results must be reported to the requestor or responsible individual
Choice 0 or more
        | RequestedPerformerType 1 What type of actor should carry out the testing
        | RequestedPerformer 1 Who should carry out the tests. For example, the patient or caregiver
ReasonForRequest optional The justification for this order, which may be because the subject is at risk for a disease, as part of a diagnostic process, due to a symptom or problem, a part of a care plan, or periodic monitoring
PerformerInstructions optional Information for the performer of the test, if needed.
PatientInstructions optional Information for the patient, such as, where to get the test, how to prepare for the test, etc.
TestCode 1 The code for a laboratory, radiologic, or other clinical test or measurement that helps to determine the presence, absence, or degree of a condition, or assess the health of an individual.
        with Coding from HIVRapidScreeningTestCodeVS Coding of a concept, drawn from a controlled vocabulary. Includes the vocabulary and version, if applicable. May include a display text, and a descriptor expressing the intended interpretation of the code
