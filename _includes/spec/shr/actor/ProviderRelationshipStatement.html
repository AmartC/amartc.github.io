ProviderRelationshipStatement [Entry]

Statement about a person or organization providing services to the person of record at a particular time or over a given time interval. Can also be used to state that a provider relationship does not exist, e.g., patient does not have a primary care provider C1314939
HealthcareRole 1 or more A type of service provided to the subject
DoesProviderRelationshipExist 1 Whether the person of record has a provider relationship of the type mentioned
Provider optional A person or organization who provides a service to the person of record. This includes healthcare and relevant non-healthcare services, and personal services such as transportation.
OccurrenceTime optional The point in time or span of time in which something happens.
