ParticipationStatement

A statement about an actor who did or did not perform a certain task or activity. Unlike a HealthcareInvolvement which continues over period of time, the performer is associated with doing or not doing a specific task, such admitting a patient, performing a procedure, or taking a measurement C1550369
Person 1 A person, particularly those relevant to the health or social situation of the subject (including the person of record).
ParticipationType optional The detail of how the performer engaged in the task, for example, as the attending physician, surgical assistant, etc.
DidOrDidNotParticipate 1 Whether the mentioned person or organization participated in the described activity
OccurrenceTime optional The point in time or span of time in which something happens.
