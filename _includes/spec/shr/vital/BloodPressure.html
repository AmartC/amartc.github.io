BloodPressure [Entry]

The force of circulating blood on the walls of the arteries. 55284-4, C0005823, 8716-3 (Vital Sign)
TestCode is 55284-4 1 The code for a laboratory, radiologic, or other clinical test or measurement that helps to determine the presence, absence, or degree of a condition, or assess the health of an individual.
SystolicPressure 1 The blood pressure during the contraction of the left ventricle of the heart, when blood pressure is at its highest
DiastolicPressure 1 The blood pressure after the contraction of the heart while the chambers of the heart refill with blood, when the pressure is lowest
