Adverse

The SHR Adverse domain contains definitions for describing adverse events and adverse reactions.

AdverseReactionStatement [Entry]

A statement about a possible adverse reaction attributed to exposure to a substance. It is not necessarily associated with a previously-recorded SubstanceRisk. This Entry could be used to record an adverse reaction to an immunization or food. Adverse reaction to substance (C0559966)

The specific code should always align with the concept of the element, but may be more specific.
