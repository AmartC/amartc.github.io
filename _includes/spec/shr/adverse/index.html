AdverseReactionToSubstance [Entry]

An instance of an adverse reaction attributed to exposure to a substance. It is not necessarily associated with a previously-recorded SubstanceRisk. This Entry could be used to record an adverse reaction to an immunization or food. C0559966 (Adverse reaction to substance)
