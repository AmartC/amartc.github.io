One human, one health record.

Building a single health record for every individual in the United States.

Our vision is to fundamentally shift how health data is digitally recorded and shared to ensure that comprehensive, high quality data is available on demand. To do this, we are architecting the Standard Health Record.

Achieving this vision and aligning disparate health data requires an agreed on format for healthcare data elements, value sets, and measurement units. Through this shared technological foundation, we can ensure clinical interoperability and better support a wide-range of clinicians, empower patients and caregivers, and provide transparency within the healthcare system.

The Standard Health Record Will Provide

  • A single, high quality source of health-related data; if it happens to the patient, it is in the SHR
  • 24/7 acessibility for patients and authorized healthcare providers to the patient’s health-record
  • A comprehensive view of the patient and their care plan to support complete patient care
  • Data transparency to support the healthcare delivery system in reducing waste, fraud, and abuse
  • Patients with a voice through the ability to add, verify, and share their data
  • A platform for innovation in big data analytics and personalized medicine
