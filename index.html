One human, one health record.

Collaboratively building a single, high-quality health record for every individual in the United States

Our vision is to fundamentally shift how health data is digitally recorded and shared to ensure that comprehensive, high quality data is available on demand.

Achieving this vision, and aligning disparate health data, requires an agreed on format for healthcare data elements, value sets, and measurement units. Through this shared technological foundation, we can solve clinical interoperability and better support a wide-range of clinicians, empower patients and caregivers, and provide transparency within the healthcare system.

The Standard Health Record (SHR) Will Provide

  • A single, high quality source of health-related data; if it happens to the patient, it is in the SHR
  • Secure, informed-consent access to patient health data across multiple platforms
  • Advanced security and privacy protocols
  • Accessibility for patients and authorized healthcare providers to the patient’s health-record 24/7, anywhere in the world
  • Empower the patient with an enduring voice by allowing them to add, verify, and easily share their data with trusted third parties
  • A robust and expandable tool to support the healthcare delivery system in reducing waste, fraud, and abuse through greater data transparency
  • Opportunities for innovation that improve the healthcare experience and associated patient outcomes
