Immunization [Entry]

A statement relating to a vaccination that was or was not administered to the subject. C0020971
Vaccine 1 A specific type or manufactured instance of a vaccine, a prophylactic or therapeutic preparation given to produce immune response to pathogenic organisms or substances.
ImmunizationNotGivenModifier 1 True, If the immunization was not given.
BodySite optional Location on or in the body.
RouteIntoBody optional The way a substance enters an organism after contact, particularly, the route of drug administration.
AmountOfMedication optional A standardized measure or discrete amount of medication serving as a reference for dosing or strength, for example, 1 tablet.
