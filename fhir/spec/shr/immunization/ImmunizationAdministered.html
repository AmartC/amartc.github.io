ImmunizationAdministered [Entry]

Represents an immunization that was administered to the subject. C0580520
Based On: Immunization A statement relating to a vaccination that was or was not administered to the subject.
Vaccine 1 A specific type or manufactured instance of a vaccine, a prophylactic or therapeutic preparation given to produce immune response to pathogenic organisms or substances.

        with LotNumber 1 A distinctive alpha-numeric identification code assigned by the manufacturer or distributor to a specific quantity of manufactured material or product within a batch.
