SHR Panel Profile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://standardhealthrecord.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shr-observation-Panel

A group of related observations, usually taken together. Typically the ObservationCategory and ObservationReason are not given for panel observations, but rather given at the level of the panel itself.

This profile builds on DiagnosticReport.

This profile was published on Thu Apr 06 11:42:38 EDT 2017 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation: Standard Health Record Collaborative.
